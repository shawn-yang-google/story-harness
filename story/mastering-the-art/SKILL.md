# Mastering the Art

Story is about **mastering the art, not second-guessing the marketplace**.

## The Marketplace Truth
- No one can teach what will sell — [FILM_INDUSTRY_CENTER]'s bombs are made with the same commercial calculation as its hits
- Darkish dramas that break every commercial "rule" (ORDINARY PEOPLE, TRAINSPOTTING) quietly conquer the box office
- Nothing in our art is guaranteed

## The Path Forward
- Put your energies into **achieving excellence** rather than agonizing over the odds
- A brilliant, original screenplay will make agents fight to represent you
- Polished, mature work **seals its integrity**; unfinished work invites tampering

## The Writer's Security
Secure writers don't sell first drafts. They patiently rewrite until the script is as director-ready, as actor-ready as possible. [FILM_INDUSTRY_CENTER]'s best talent is acutely aware that their careers depend on working within quality writing.
