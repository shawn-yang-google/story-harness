# Antiplot — Anti-Structure

The cinema counterpart to the anti-novel (Nouveau Roman) and Theatre of the Absurd.

## How It Works
Antiplot doesn't **reduce** the Classical — it **reverses** it, contradicting traditional forms to exploit, perhaps ridicule the very idea of formal principles.

## Key Reversals
| Classical Principle | Antiplot Reversal |
|-------------------|-------------------|
| Causality | **Coincidence** — events connected by chance |
| Linear time | **Nonlinear time** — fragmented chronology |
| Consistent reality | **Inconsistent realities** — shifts between worlds |

## Character
The Antiplot-maker is rarely interested in understatement or quiet austerity. To make clear his "revolutionary" ambitions, films tend toward **extravagance and self-conscious overstatement**.

UN CHIEN ANDALOU, [CLASSIC_FILM], PERSONA, MONTY PYTHON AND THE HOLY GRAIL, STRANGER THAN PARADISE, WAYNE'S WORLD, CHUNGKING EXPRESS, LOST HIGHWAY.

## Note
Predominantly **European and post-World War II**, though with notable American and Asian examples.
