# Archplot — Classical Design

The collection of timeless principles [MASTER_THEORIST] calls the Archplot ("eminent above others of the same kind").

## Definition
A story built around an **active protagonist** who struggles against primarily **external forces of antagonism** to pursue desire, through **continuous time**, within a **consistent and causally connected** fictional reality, to a **closed ending** of absolute, irreversible change.

## Characteristics
| Element | Archplot |
|---------|----------|
| Protagonist | Single, active |
| Conflict | Primarily external |
| Time | Linear, continuous |
| Reality | Consistent, causal |
| Ending | Closed — all questions answered |
| Change | Absolute, irreversible |

## Why It Dominates
These principles are **timeless and transcultural**, fundamental to every earthly society. When Gilgamesh was carved in cuneiform 4,000 years ago, Classical Design was already fully in place.

## Examples
THE GODFATHER, CITIZEN KANE, THE SEVEN SAMURAI, CASABLANCA, THELMA & LOUISE, SHINE, DIE HARD — staggering variety within one form.
