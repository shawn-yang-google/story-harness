# Thoroughness in Craft

Story is about **thoroughness, not shortcuts**.

## The Paradox of Economy
- Screen and prose writers create the same density of world, character, and story
- Screenplay pages have much white space, misleading us into thinking they're quicker than novels
- Film writers cut and cut again, ruthless in their desire to express the **absolute maximum in the fewest possible words**

## [PHILOSOPHER]'s Lesson
> [PHILOSOPHER] once wrote a long, drawn-out letter to a friend, then apologized in the postscript that he didn't have time to write a short one.

Like [PHILOSOPHER], screenwriters learn that:
- **Economy is key**
- **Brevity takes time**
- **Excellence means perseverance**
