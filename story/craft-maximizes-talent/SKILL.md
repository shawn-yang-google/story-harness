# Craft Maximizes Talent

> Talent without craft is like fuel without an engine. It burns wildly but accomplishes nothing.

## The Coffee Machine Test
Given the choice between **trivial material brilliantly told** versus **profound material badly told**, an audience will always choose the trivial told brilliantly.

- The master storyteller can make "How I Put My Kids on the School Bus" riveting
- The poor storyteller can make the death of a mother boring
- Master storytellers squeeze life from the least of things; poor storytellers reduce the profound to the banal

## How Craft Works
1. You enter your imagined world and **write**
2. You step out and **read** what you've written
3. You **analyze**: "Is it good? Does it work? Why not?"
4. Write, read; create, critique; impulse, logic; right brain, left brain

The quality of your rewriting depends on **command of craft** that guides you to correct imperfection.

## The Liberation
When the conscious mind is put to work on the objective task of executing craft, the **spontaneous surfaces**. Mastery of craft frees the subconscious. An artist is never at the mercy of impulse — he exercises craft to create harmonies of instinct and idea.
