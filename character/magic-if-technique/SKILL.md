# Magic If Technique

Apply [METHOD_THEORIST]’s "Magic If" to inhabit your character’s consciousness and generate authentic reactions.

### Key Principles

- **Inhabit the Character**: Ask "If I were this character in this situation, what would I do?" instead of "What would my character do?"
- **Project Consciousness**: See through the character's eyes, hear with their ears, and sense their environment as if their fictional life were happening to you.
- **Trigger Authentic Emotion**: Let emotions flow from the character's perspective rather than your own guesses.
- **Act Out Scenes**: Use your own mind and energy to drive character creation by improvising their choices moment by moment.
- **Leverage Self-Knowledge**: Understand your own true nature to better perceive your character’s complexities.
- **Bridge Inner Lives**: Occupy the character's mind so that your self-awareness and their self-awareness act as one.
