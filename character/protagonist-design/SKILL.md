# Protagonist Design

Construct a compelling central figure by integrating essential qualities that drive the story's progression.

### Essential Qualities

- **Willpower**: Grant the protagonist the resolve to pursue an object of desire to its absolute limit, especially at the final crisis.
- **Multiple Capabilities**: Ensure they possess the mental, emotional, and physical traits necessary to make their actions credible and impactful.
- **Underdog Status**: Pit them against antagonistic forces that virtually overwhelm them, making success a hard-won chance.
- **Empathic Nature**: Position them as the story's "Center of Good" to attract reader identification.
- **Complexity and Intrigue**: Include contradictory qualities that force the reader to wonder who the character truly is.
- **Capacity for Change**: Allow the protagonist to arc through reform, learning, degeneration, or disillusionment.
- **Insight and Epiphany**: Lead them to a mind-blazing truth or self-recognition that pivots their life.

### Design Variations

- **Co-Protagonists**: Join two contrasting characters into a multifaceted team.
- **Group Protagonists**: Unite a collection of characters who share a singular desire and mutual consequences.
- **Passive Protagonists**: Focus on an invisible inner struggle where unexpressed thoughts rarely convert to action.
- **Switching Protagonists**: Jolt the story direction by shifting the protagonist role mid-telling.
