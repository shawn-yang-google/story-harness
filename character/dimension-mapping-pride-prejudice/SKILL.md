# Dimension Mapping: [REGENCY_ROMANCE]

[SOCIAL_NOVELIST]'s cast design places the complex [SOCIAL_HEROINE] at center, surrounded by four flat sisters whose single traits each mirror and activate one of [SOCIAL_HEROINE]'s dimensions.

### [SOCIAL_HEROINE]'s Four Dimensions

1. **Rational / Impulsive**: Patient, mature insights into people — until prejudicial judgment about [PROUD_SUITOR] contradicts her normally balanced acumen.
2. **Social Charm / Private Conviction**: Deep ethical beliefs hidden behind smiling charisma rather than bore or antagonize.
3. **Self-Esteem / Humility**: Spirited sense of self-worth restrained by unaffected modesty.
4. **Independence / Romantic Longing**: Willful independence contradicted by equally strong attraction to [PROUD_SUITOR].

### The Four Sister Counterpoints

- **[INNOCENT_SISTER]** (Innocent): Naïvely trusts in the goodness of people — sharply diverges from [SOCIAL_HEROINE]'s sophisticated skepticism, activating Dimension 1.
- **[PEDANTIC_SISTER]** (Pedantic): Irks people with moralizing pretentiousness — contrasts with [SOCIAL_HEROINE]'s ability to hold convictions behind charm, activating Dimension 2.
- **[IMMODEST_SISTER]** (Immodest): Puts animal spirits on constant display — opposes [SOCIAL_HEROINE]'s restrained modesty, activating Dimension 3.
- **[DEPENDENT_SISTER]** (Dependent): Weak-willed, needy, easily reduced to tears — opposes [SOCIAL_HEROINE]'s willful independence, activating Dimension 4.

### The [PROUD_SUITOR] Mirror

[PROUD_SUITOR] adds three dimensions (Prideful/Chastened, Arrogant/Kindhearted, Self-deceived/Self-aware). [SOCIAL_HEROINE]'s intelligent calm contradicts the frantic insecurities of all four sisters, keeping her solidly at the novel's center.

### Application

- Use this pattern: give the protagonist multiple dimensions, then create flat counterpoint characters whose single traits each illuminate one dimension.
- The more dimensions in a character, the more relationships needed to delineate complexities.
- Map traits and dimensions on a cast map to verify each role activates a specific contrast.
