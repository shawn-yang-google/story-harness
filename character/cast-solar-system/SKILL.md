# Cast Solar System

Design the gravitational hierarchy of your story's cast by organizing roles into concentric rings around the protagonist.

### The Solar System Model

- **The Sun**: Place the protagonist at the absolute center as the star role that draws the most interest and time.
- **First Circle**: Position major characters who orbit closest. These characters help or hinder the protagonist and have the potential for stories of their own.
- **Second Circle**: Place supporting roles that help or hinder the plot but lack the magnetism to interest the reader beyond their specific scenes.
- **Third Circle**: Populate the outer reaches with minor roles, bit parts, and background masses that provide social density and exposition.

### Design Rules

- **Strategic Arrangement**: Ensure no two characters occupy the same position or perform the same tasks in the same way.
- **Gravitational Influence**: Calibrate character impact based on their proximity to the protagonist.
- **Establish Relationships**: Build a complex network of interconnections that define the social universe of the story.
