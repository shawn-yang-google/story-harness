# Writing in Character

Turn your inner life into your character’s inner life through deep empathetic inhabitation and improvisation.

### Key Principles

- **Become the First Actor**: Treat yourself as the character’s primary performer, experiencing what they experience in real-time.
- **Improvise Choices**: Pace, wave, and speak aloud to act out your creations—man, woman, child, or monster.
- **Occupy the Senses**: Live inside the character’s senses so that story events happen *in* you as much as they happen to the character.
- **Embrace Subjectivity**: Jump from your own consciousness into the character’s inner self to achieve truthfulness and subtlety.
- **Utilize Self-Insight**: Use your understanding of your own rock-bottom inner self as the base for exploring a character's hidden depths.
- **Sync Minds**: Ensure your mind occupies the character's mind until your thoughts and their awareness act as a unified force.
