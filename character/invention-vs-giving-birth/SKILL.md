# Invention vs. Giving Birth

Understand the two primary modes of character creation and how they work together.

- **Conscious Invention**:
    - Start with a story inspiration or a real-life behavior.
    - Brainstorm traits and dimensions that fulfill your original vision.
    - Ask "What kind of person would do this?" to build psychological depth.
- **Giving Birth (Subconscious Manifestation)**:
    - Act as a conduit for personalities that emerge organically.
    - Allow characters to reveal themselves slowly through stray scenes or bits of dialogue.
    - Wait for characters to "do their little things" and keep what feels truthful.
- Combine both modes: use subconscious surprises and conscious improvisation to build characters.
- Recognize that characters are works of art, not artists; they manifest in your mind and follow your imagination.
- Trust your subconscious to absorb experiences and research, then stir them into new forms.
- Stay wide-awake and conscious while giving breath to what you already know.
