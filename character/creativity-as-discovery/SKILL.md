# Creativity as Discovery

Employ creativity as the discovery of the "Third Thing" by fusing unrelated elements.

- Act as a duality-seeking sensor to spot hidden analogies between existing things.
- Merge two known things into an utterly new, never-seen-before fusion (the Third Thing).
- Facilitate the flow of genius between the left brain (logic, patterns) and the right brain (analogy, intuition).
- Use Carl Sandburg’s "Fog" (Fog + Cat = stillness) as a model for metaphorical discovery.
- Alternate directions of discovery:
    - **Conceptual to Actual**: Start with an archetype and bring it down to earth (e.g., *[SUPERHERO_WORK]*).
    - **Actual to Conceptual**: Start with a real-life event and elevate it to a symbolic scale (e.g., *[FILM_DRAMA]*).
- Allow the right brain to seize random thoughts and the left brain to fit them into your character sketches.
- Tame real-life chaos with aesthetic order through the collaboration of reason and imagination.
