# Four Selves Model

Layer your character's behavior across four distinct realms of identity to create multifaceted humanity.

### The Four Realms

- **Private Realm**: Focus on the core self coping with inner dilemmas and making decisions in isolation. Maintain a stable sense of identity while the stream of consciousness evolves.
- **[CLASSIC_FILM]l Realm**: Adapt the agent self to intimate relationships (family, friends, lovers). Vary gestures, tones, and energies based on the degree of intimacy and balance of power.
- **Social Realm**: Employ social masks to navigate public hierarchies and institutions. Use tactful personae to avoid friction while pursuing goals in professional or public settings.
- **Subconscious Realm**: Acknowledge the hidden self driven by survival imperatives. Integrate unperceived perceptions, habits, dreams, and contradictory desires that influence conscious behavior.

### Implementation

- **Map Relationships**: Design unique behaviors for each relationship context the character enters.
- **Balance Contradictions**: Use the subconscious to drive choices that the character may not consciously understand.
- **Show Identity Evolution**: Inherit identity from culture/parents but evolve through the differences that emerge over time.
