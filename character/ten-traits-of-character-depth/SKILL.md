# Ten Traits of Character Depth

Human beings have an inborn sonar that pings the moment they meet someone — First Impression. Characters with inner dimensions reaching into private and hidden selves encourage empathy and trust.

### The Ten Traits

1. **Ironic Self-Awareness**: Always on guard against self-deception, rarely fooled by what goes on inside his own mind. ([POLITICAL_PARTNER] in *[POLITICAL_DRAMA]*)
2. **Insight into Others**: Always aware of public pretensions, never fooled by society's performances. ([MANIPULATIVE_CONFIDANTE] in *[CLASSIC_NOVEL]*)
3. **Intelligence**: Can think. Gathers knowledge from all disciplines and commands the logic to use it. ([ELDERLY_DETECTIVE] in Agatha Christie)
4. **A History of Suffering**: Depth comes through pain. The suffering mind sinks deeper into itself, smashing through floors of the core self to reveal new levels. ([FIXER_ENFORCER] in *[CRIME_DRAMA_SERIES]*)
5. **Broad Experience over Time**: Depth needs wide-ranging experience and, above all, time. ([AGING_NARRATOR] in *[HISTORICAL_NOVEL]*)
6. **Focused Attention**: Listens intently face-to-face, makes eye contact, reads subtext. (Don [MOB_PATRIARCH] in *[MAFIA_EPIC]*)
7. **Love of Beauty**: Depth sharpens sensitivity until beauty is almost painful. ([SENSITIVE_RECLUSE] in *[MODERN_NOVEL]*)
8. **Calm**: No matter the threat or stress, keeps passions under control. ([OLD_COWBOY] in *[WESTERN_NOVEL]*)
9. **Cynic and Skeptic**: Hope is the denial of reality. Doesn't trust anything until tested against his own mind. ([STRONG_SURVIVOR] in *[MODERN_PLAY]*)
10. **A Searcher for Meaning**: Gets God's joke — knows life has no intrinsic meaning, so searches for one in the gap between living for yourself and living for others. ([SEARCHING_WOMAN] in *[MODERN_NOVEL]*)

### The Antihero Composite

Roll these ten traits into one role and they often create an antihero: a loner hardened by bad luck but vulnerable to others' suffering; stoic yet self-mocking; cynical about rules but committed to a personal code; a romantic wary of romance.

### Application

- Use this list as a checklist when building complex protagonists. Not every trait is needed, but the more you embed, the deeper the audience's trust and empathy.
